{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} This is a simple extension that adds the ability to build and upload mod lists directly to Modwatch ([modwat.ch](https://modwat.ch)) from your Mods list in Vortex, powered by Vortex Showcase. **This project is unofficial!** We have no association with Nexus Mods or Modwatch and this is only a community project. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="dark" >}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-tools" title="Full Vortex integration" %}} Easily upload your modlist directly from your installed Vortex mods. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fab fa-github" title="Contributions welcome!" url="https://github.com/agc93/vortex-showcase" %}} The extension is fully open-source and open to community contributions and fixes! {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-cogs" title="Seamless" %}} Doesn't require the Modwatch Uploader to be installed (or any external tools) and will upload instantly. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/section >}}
